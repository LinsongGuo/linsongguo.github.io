I am a Ph.D student at University of California San Diego. I am very fortunate to work with Professor Amy Ousterhout. My current research topic is preemptive user-space scheduling.

I received my bachelor's degree in computer science from ACM Honored Class of Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU). During my undergraduate, I interned at Professor Dong Xie's Database System Group at Penn State University, and researched on serverless computing in SJTU EPCC lab, advised by Professor Quan Chen. Also, I won three gold medals (one first runner-up) in ACM-ICPC Asia regional contests.
